What is Computer Networking?

Computer Networking is the discipline that enables the interconnection of computing devices to share data, applications, and resources efficiently. It forms the backbone of communication systems worldwide, from small-scale LANs to the vast global Internet. Networking includes both hardware (like routers, switches, and cables) and software (protocols and services). Over the decades, it has grown to support innovations such as cloud computing, IoT, 5G, and edge computing. A strong understanding of networking helps engineers build resilient and scalable digital infrastructures.
